Frequently Asked Questions

When do I get paid? ‚ñº
Payouts are processed automatically on the 1st of each month. Funds typically arrive in your bank account within 2-3 business days. You can also request an early payout from your dashboard if your balance exceeds $50.
What's the revenue split? ‚ñº
You keep 85% of all earnings from subscriptions, tips, and pay-per-view content. Uncaged takes 15%. Stripe's processing fee (2.9% + $0.30) is deducted from the total before the split.
Which countries are supported? ‚ñº
Stripe Connect supports creators in 40+ countries including the US, Canada, UK, most of Europe, Australia, and many others. During the connection process, Stripe will confirm if your country is supported.
Is my information secure? ‚ñº
Yes. Stripe is a PCI Level 1 certified payment processor (the highest security standard). Your bank details are encrypted and stored by Stripe, not by Uncaged. We never see your full account numbers.
What information do I need to provide? ‚ñº
Stripe will ask for: your legal name, date of birth, address, last 4 digits of SSN (US) or equivalent ID, and bank account details. This is required by financial regulations to verify your identity.
