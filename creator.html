About Visa Vic

Hey, I'm Vic - a military veteran turned travel content creator. After years of being told where to go and what to do, I decided to take control of my life and see the world on my own terms.

I started documenting my travels in 2023, focusing on the experiences that mainstream travel content ignores - the nightlife, the dating culture, the real interactions with locals. YouTube kept restricting my content, so I built UNCAGED to share these stories without limitations.

My mission is simple: show you the world as it really is, not the sanitized version you see on traditional platforms. From the favelas of Rio to the clubs of Cape Town, I'm bringing you along for the ride.

When I'm not traveling, I'm working on building businesses and creating content that helps other men live life on their own terms.
